About Anvera

Built from real
operational experience.

Anvera was built around a simple idea: small businesses should not have to run on scattered tools, manual reporting, and constant guesswork to understand how they are performing.

Founder

Meet New Angel.

I’m New Angel Ramirez Dueñas, founder of Anvera. I started building these systems after working closely with a boutique hotel and seeing how difficult it can be to manage reservations, finances, reporting, and daily decisions across disconnected tools.

That experience made one thing clear: many small businesses are working hard, but they still do not have one clear system that shows what is happening, what needs attention, and where to focus next.

I study Finance and Economics with a focus on Data Science, and I care about building systems that are practical, maintainable, and grounded in how a business actually operates day to day. That is the mindset behind Anvera.

Why it exists

Better visibility for smaller operators.

Anvera is for businesses that need a clearer way to track performance, manage workflows, and make day-to-day decisions without relying on scattered spreadsheets and manual follow-up.

How it works

Clarity before complexity.

The goal is not to overload a business with software. The goal is to build the right level of system, with the right information, in a way that is actually usable.

Principles

What matters most.

  • Less manual work wherever possible
  • Clear information that supports action
  • Systems that can evolve with the business
  • Practical structure before unnecessary complexity
Core tools

Built with practical tools.

  • Google Sheets and Apps Script
  • Python and Google Colab
  • Custom web interfaces
  • GitHub-based deployment and iteration
